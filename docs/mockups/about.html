About

Local knowledge, real trails

EnduroFun is a small team of riders and guides based in Álora, running multi-day enduro routes across the wildest parts of the province of Málaga.

Our story

Riders first, guides by trade

What started as a handful of friends exploring the trails around Álora on weekends became a small guided-tour operation, built on years of riding the same terrain in every season and condition — placeholder copy, replaced with real content when the client supplies it.

What makes us different

Three things riders notice

Local knowledge

Routes built from years riding this exact terrain, not GPS tracks pulled off the internet.

Varied terrain

Dusty singletrack, rocky climbs and open fire roads, all within a short ride of Álora.

Cultural offering

Rest-day options like Caminito del Rey or Málaga's old town — more than just a bike trip.

Experience levels

Who this is for

Beginner

Comfortable on a bike, new to off-road — routes chosen with easier sections and more guidance.

Intermediate

Some enduro/off-road experience — the standard route mix on most packages.

Advanced

Confident on technical terrain — routes can be adjusted for more demanding sections on request.
